  • 1934

    The first local Committee is formed

  • 1975

    Fund our first Macmillan nurse and build the first Macmillan Cancer Care unit

  • 1980

    Invest £2.5million to expand Macmillan nursing teams throughout the UK.

    Macmillan launch an educational programme to train doctors and nurses as well as students in advanced pain control and cancer care.

  • 1986

    We fund the first Macmillan doctor

  • 1989

    Macmillan change their name from Society for the Prevention and Relief of Cancer to Cancer Relief Macmillan Fund

  • 1991

    Macmillan launch the Macmillan Nurse Appeal to raise £20million

    Macmillan launch the annual Worlds Biggest Coffee Morning. 300,000 people take part raising over £250,000

  • 1997

    Macmillan change their name from Cancer Relief Macmillan Fund to Macmillan Cancer Relief

  • 1998

    Open the new Macmillan National Institute of Education (MNIE) with 10 lecturers for training specialist cancer care professionals

    The Information Line helps more than 11,000 people during its first year of operation.

  • 1999

    Macmillan successfully lobbies the Oxford English Dictionary to change its inaccurate definition of cancer

    Macmillan merges with Cancerlink

    Our first mobile cancer information centre visits 51 sities across England.

    Macmillan finish our 100th building project, a day centre at Craigavon Northern Ireland.

  • 2002

    Macmillan CancerLine is launched, integrating the existing Macmillan and CancerLink information services

  • 2003

    Macmillan implement their social care strategy, focusing on supporting carers and helping people financially

  • 2006

    Macmillan change their name to Macmillan Cancer Support

  • 2008

    We merge with CancerBackup allowing us to provide high quality cancer information

  • 2009

    Our integrated phone service launches allowing people affected by cancer to call just one number for medical, financial, practical and emotional support

    Number of Macmillan professionals rises to over 5,000

  • 2011

    Macmillan celebrate their centenary with a star-studded gala.

    Another Worlds Biggest Coffee Morning raises more than £10million.

    Publish research showing number of people with cancer will double from 2 million to 4 million by 2030/

  • 2012

    Macmillan's services reach a record 5.7 million people

  • 2013

    Macmillan once again voted number one charity in the Brand Index

    Launch their award winning 'Not Alone' campaign

  • 2014

    Macmillan becomes The Marketing Society's brand of the year
